About me

Front-end developer from Montreal, Canada

I’ve always been drawn to the creative side of technology, and front-end development quickly became the perfect blend of logic and design for me. What started as a simple interest soon turned into a passion for building clean, interactive, and user-focused websites. Along the way, I’ve honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React—bringing ideas to life through smooth interfaces and responsive design. I love experimenting with new tools, from integrating APIs to exploring AI-driven features, always looking for ways to make the web more dynamic and accessible. Every project I take on is a chance to learn something new and craft a better user experience.
